About

I am a graduate student at Politecnico di Milano, pursuing a MSc in Mathematical Engineering and majoring in Computational Science and Computational Learning. I have previously earned a BSc in Mathematical Engineering at the same university. My interests lie in the fields of Numerical Mathematics and Deep Learning.

Projects

During the last years I've worked on:

  • Implementing uncertainty quantification methods for neural ordinary differential equations and building parametrized neural ODEs. Presented at the Computational Statistics Course Workshop, Politecnico di Milano (code, slides).
  • The development of tinyrand, a (tiny) library for pseudo-random number generation.
