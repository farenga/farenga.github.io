About

I am a graduate student at Politecnico di Milano, pursuing a MSc in Mathematical Engineering and majoring in Computational Science and Computational Learning. I have previously earned a BSc in Mathematical Engineering at the same university. My interests lie in the fields of Scientific Computing and Deep Learning.

Projects

Some of the projects I've worked on include:

  • The development of Graph Neural Networks-based methods to perform efficient mesh agglomeration, and their implementation together with multigrid solvers. (code, report).
  • The implementation of Uncertainty Quantification methods for Neural Ordinary Differential Equations, and the design of parameterized NODEs models to perform Model Order Reduction. (code, slides).
  • The development of tinyrand, a (tiny) library for pseudo-random number generation.
